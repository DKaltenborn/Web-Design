Walks

Taking your dog for a walk is one of the simplest ways fo them to get exercise. All dogs, no matter the breed, like going on walks. Whether its at a park or around the block, it gets you out of the house and it makes them tired.

Runs

If you're a more athletic owner, maybe running is more your speed? Or maybe you just want to shoot of some balls and let your dog sprint across a wide open field. Either way, it's a great way to get your dog exercise. Just remember, dogs aren't made to be long distance runners, so keep the run to something manageable and always bring water.

Sports

There are many sports that dogs can do. In the professional world, some of these sports are herding, agility, rally, Shutzhund. For regular people, they're just fancy ways to say you play with your dog in a way that trains their body and mind. There's many different ways to exercise your dog and how you do that is up to you.

Benefits

The benefits of giving your dog exercise are that the two of you get to bond and your dog gets all their rambunctious, destructive energy out of their system. A tired dog is a good dog.
